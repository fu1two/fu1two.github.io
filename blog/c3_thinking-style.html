The Life of an Abstract Random

4.26.15

It is a bit eerie how accurately this mind style describes me. And at the same time oddly comforting that I fit into one of four categories.

I believe it is good to be aware of the positives and negatives that come with my particular style. I do feel that I am good at mediating and keeping the peace. I will always hear out everyone's ideas and at the same time keep the team focused on the task at hand.

I do feel that some of the negatives that come with this mind style do apply to me. I certainly have trouble working with strong personalities. I do have trouble focusing on one thing at a time even though I can focus on the issues at hand. I do, occasionally, have trouble giving exact details. A few more from the list apply to me, but knowing about them know gives me an edge. I have the ability to be mindful of these negatives. This will allow me to either work around or work through them when it comes to collaborative work.

With this new mindfulness pertaining to my mind style I'll have taken on a more growth like mindset. Which is the end will help my internal processes evolve.
