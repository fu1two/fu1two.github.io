Values 5.24.15

Values:

Accomplishment: 5

Close Relationships: 5

Helping Others: 5

Mentorship: 5

Compassion: 5

Integrity: 5

And more!

I have always felt that helping others is one of the most important things that you can ever do in your life. The whole idea of making someone’s life easier is one that makes me happy. In many ways this may sound selfish, but it is true nonetheless. I do truly like to help people, though. Knowing that the person I helped will have a better day because of something I’ve done is awesome.

The most recent advice I gave to someone had to do with a difficult manager. I would say that in this case it would most relate to compassion. That the manager may be going through difficult times and that is why they are acting the way they are. And to try to maintain a certain level of compassion for their manager. And that with this they would be able to better understand the actions of the manager.

Thinking about my values makes me feel that I have a purpose, that all of these qualities define me and will overtake any potentially negative aspects of myself. I think all my values combined are what will help mitigate any stereotype threat that I feel. Like I said before all these positive values that I know define me will be able to overtake anything negative such as stereotype threat.
